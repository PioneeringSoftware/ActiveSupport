27 /**
28  * Transforms words from singular to plural and vice versa, transforms
29  * lowercase and underscored words to human-readable form, etc.
30  *
31  * ASInflector departs somewhat with Rails in its layout. Rails uses
32  * ActiveSupport and Inflector as nested modules, neither as classes. Rails also
33  * uses Inflections as the primary class for capturing the inflection
34  * behaviour. The module Inflector defines methods for accessing the default
35  * inflections instance. Objective-C has nothing quite like modules. Hence
36  * ASInflector encapsulates the primary behaviour. You can access the shared
37  * inflector using +[ASInflector defaultInflector].
